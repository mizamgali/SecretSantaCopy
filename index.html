Questions and answers about service

? What is Secret Santa?

Secret Santa is a popular anonymous gift exchange ceremony. This is a New Year's game with simple rules: each participant is a Secret Santa for one of the other participants and secretly prepares a gift for him. As a result, everyone gets both the joy of preparing a surprise and a gift. This site is a service that helps organize this game online.
